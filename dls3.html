SCENARIO 3:Interruption to Team 1's innings
In this scenario,team 1's innings is interrupted due to rain , and since the rain persisted for a longer duration of time , the match referees had no option but to terminate team 1's innings and set a revised target for team 2 depending on the overs of play possible. NOTE:The average T20 score considered here in calculations is 166 although it largely depends on the conditions and the ground dimensions. The actual calculation considers the average T20 score at the venue where the match is conducted. (Use this scenario if you want to calculate the revised target for the team 2)
